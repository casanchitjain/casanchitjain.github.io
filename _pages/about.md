I am a PhD Candidate in Finance & Accounting at the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore.

My research focuses on corporate finance, financial intermediation, mutual funds, regulatory design, and text analysis. My dissertation, *Signal, Structure, and Outcome: Essays on Information Transmission in Mutual Funds and Business Groups in India*, examines how information affects market outcomes in emerging markets.

Before beginning my doctoral studies, I worked in wealth management and asset management and qualified as a Chartered Accountant.

My work has been published in the *Journal of Futures Markets* and in teaching case outlets including Harvard Business Publishing.
